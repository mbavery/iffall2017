Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Released: 1984
Starring: Sumi Shimamoto, GoroōNaya, Yoji Matsuda, Yoshiko Sakakibara, Iemasa Kayumi

Based on Hayao Miyazaki 1982 manga of the same name, Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind takes place in a future post-apocalyptic world, telling the story of Nausicaä (Shimamoto), the young princess of the Valley of the Wind. She becomes embroiled in a struggle with Tolmekia, a kingdom that tries to use an ancient weapon to eradicate a jungle of mutant giant insects.
